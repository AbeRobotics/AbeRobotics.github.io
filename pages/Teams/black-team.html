About Allen Keys

Our team started building the robot on September 21, 2022. The main goal for the Allen Keys/Team Black is to reach the winning alliance this year. In regards to the robot, we're trying to develop a highly functional claw.

Abou Allen Keys Team Captain

My name is Sebastian and I am the captain of Team Black. Personally, I'm interested in robotics for the teamwork, building skills, the fun of working with the robot, and developing problem solving skills. We have been making excellent progress with our robot and are improving the many facets of our design. From the excellent claw design to the small frame we have been constantly searching for ways to improve and develop our robot so it can be a strong competitor.
