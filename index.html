    William Aberhart FTC Team

Who are we?

The Aberhart Robotics Republic (ARR) is William Aberhart High School's First Robotics FTC team. The team spans all grades with many skill levels, and consists of a White and Orange Team. We look to emphasize the skills of each individual, whether that be programming, engineering, design, or photography. We will be compete in FIRST Tech Challenges. We work on our robots every Wednesday in our school's cafeteria. We are proud recipients of the FTC 2019-20 "Skystone" Collins Aerospace Innovate Award.

What is FTC?

FTC stands for FIRST Tech Challenge. It is a competition designed for students in grades 7-12. Teams are given 10 weeks to build and program a robot designed for the year's challenge.

Our Robots

Both robots are assembled using FTC Tetrix Build Kits, with REV Robotics Expansion Hubs, Mecanum wheels, Torquenado motors (with encoders), and our award-winning sponges. They use an articulated arm to grab and stack up to 7 blocks high. They use rotating claws to pull the foundation.
