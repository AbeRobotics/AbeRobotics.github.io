    William Aberhart FTC Team

Who are we?

The Aberhart Robotics Republic (ARR) is William Aberhart's First Robotics FTC team. The team spans all grades with many variations of skill levels, and consists of an ARR Orange team and an ARR White team. We look to emphasize the skills of each individual, whether that be programming, engineering, design, or photography. We will be again be competing in the FIRST Robotics FTC 2019-20 'Skystone' Challenge with two competition robots we design and program. This is a place where students get to be challenged but also have fun! Each week we come together to share our love for all things tech and bond over the joy of building robots.

What is FTC?

FTC stands for FIRST Tech Challenge. It is a competition designed for students in grades 7-12 and places them in head to head competition with one another. Teams are given a 10 week period to build the robot that they will compete with. The challenge is designed to engage kids in programming and mechanical thinking. The impact of involvement in first is well documented. First students are more involved in school and preferred by universities. However, the most important part of FIRST is being part of a team and community and bonding with likeminded students.

The Robots

These robots are the fruits of our labor. They are a collaborative effort between all students of the club working together to create something meaningful and exciting.

ARR Team White Robot

VEX

This is the Aberhart White Team's competion robot for the 2018-2019 season. This robot's main objective is to lift itself up and using it's pull up arm to secure a large amount of points. It can also transfer minerals to the depot as well as perform several tasks during the autonomous period. It also has image recognition capabilities!

ARR Team Orange Robot

This is the Aberhart Orange Team's competion robot for the 2018-2019 season. This robot also focuses primarily on scoring points by doing a pull up. However, the design of this robot's arm is slightly different. It uses it's extendable arm to retrieve place minerals in the depot.
