About Alpha

Our team started building the robot on September 21, 2022. During our first meeting, we talked about general design philosophy ideas, for example; we reviewed the game and came up with a general plan for the future of our robot. Robot development started the following week with the disassembly of last year's robot and the construction of the frame. Our main goal is to create a robot that plays the game in the most efficient manner whilst using mostly self-designed parts and mechanisms. are focusing on using CAD in the development of individual aspects of the robot, as we have access to 3D printing technology and would like to take advantage of the resources we have. In the past, we have had trouble with the completion of the robot, so we have implemented SCRUM structure into the development.

About Alpha Team Captain

My name is Matthias and I am the captain of Team Alpha. I am personally interested in robotics as it provides an opportunity to develop my leadership, communication and STEM-related skills. I have always enjoyed learning how things work, and the technology we have access to in this program allows me to learn more about the engineering stage of development for most products. We have grown a lot as a team, and our reach and influence in our school community just keeps growing. I hope that our future members continue this upward trend as the introduction to the STEM industry in education continues to grow.
