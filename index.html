    William Aberhart FTC Team

Who are we ?

The Aberhart Robotics Republic (ARR) is William Aberhart's First Robotics FTC team. The team spans all grades with many variations of skill levels, and consists of an ARR Orange team and an ARR White team. We look to emphasize the skills of each individual, whether that be programming, engineering, design, or photography. We will be again be competing in the First Tech 2018 'Rover Ruckus' Challenge with two competition robots we design and program. This is a place where students get to be challenged but also have fun! Each week we come together to share our love for all things tech and bond over the joy of building robots.

What is FTC?

FTC stands for FIRST Tech Challenge. It is a competition designed for students in grades 7-12 and places them in head to head competition with one another. Teams are given a 10 week period to build the robot that they will compete with. The challenge is designed to engage kids in programming and mechanical thinking. The impact of involvement in first is well documented. First students are more involved in school and preferred by universities. However, the most important part of FIRST is being part of a team and community and bonding with likeminded students.

The Robots

These robots are the fruits of our labor. They are a collaborative effort between all students of the club working together to create something meaningful and exciting.

VEX Robot

VEX

The VEX Robot is our official pet robot, and is primarily used to challenge the competition robot during practices for the game. The VEX is equipped with a claw-like arm which gives it the ability to pick up and move objects with ease, and is also simple to maneuver. For us, Vexy always brings joy and laughter to every meet!

Competition Robot

FTC Tetrix Build Kit

This is our official ARR Orange competition robot, and is the one that will be competiting in the FTC competition. It is constantly in the stages of designing and rebuilding so that it can complete the challenges outlined in the game. Some of it's capabilities include pushing, picking up, and lifting objects, as well as knocking things over with its arm! It also has image recognition capabilities!

Second Competition Robot

REV Robotics

Our Tetrix robot finally has a friend to play with! Welcome our newest REV robot to the family! This is the primary competition robot for our ARR White team, and will also be competing in the FTC regional championship. It has a pretty awesome arm with a claw that can pick up and lift various objects!
